About the Camp

This camp is designed to introduce students to real-world engineering through hands-on application. The program focuses on building foundational skills in electronics, mechanics, logical thinking, and system integration by engaging students in practical projects.

🛠️ Hands-on Learning
🧠 Logical Thinking

5-Day Learning Journey

Participants progress through a structured journey—from understanding basic circuits and mechanical motion to developing algorithmic control and integrating intelligent systems. The camp culminates in a final showcase where students present and explain their working models, reinforcing both technical understanding and communication skills.

Day 1

⚡ The Spark

Understanding current, circuits and how machines get power.

Day 2

⚙️ Motion

Mechanics, movement, stability and structure design.

Day 3

🧠 Control

Algorithms and logic that control machines.

Day 4

🤖 Integration

Sensors, automation and intelligent systems.

Day 5

🎤 Showcase

Students present their projects and explain their logic.
